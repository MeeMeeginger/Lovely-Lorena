Inspiration
Chinese New Year 2018 “Year of the Dog” celebration begins today, February 16 and goes thru March 2nd.  The New Year starts each year on the second new moon after the
winter solstice
(shortest day of the year) and continues to the full moon.
Dogs are the eleventh sign of the zodiac
.  I feel a special connection to the year of the Dog (besides I do like dogs).  The Chinese would say, the dogs are sincere, loyal, independent, and decisive, who aren’t afraid of life’s difficulties. Because of these characteristics they also enjoy harmonious relationships with those around them. Traits which I have strived for and I believe I’ve succeeded in accomplishing in my life along with other traits such as a genuine passion for what I do.
The celebrations and activities are abundant with many vibrant colors, lighted lanterns, dragons, dancing, fire crackers and so much more.  Just use your search engine to look for Chinese New Year activities.  You may be amazed at how many there are.
I’ve always enjoyed the excitement, atmosphere, brilliant colors and flowers, and displays surrounding the Chinese New Year celebration.  Come out and enjoy some of the activities.  I’ll be out there as much as I can, so say hi when you see me.
